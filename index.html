Uma Gunturi

I am currently a Machine Learning Engineer in IBM Watsonx Assistant working on expressive neural voices for our Text to Speech service. You can check out some of the voices I helped build here. Previously, I worked on the IBM Watsonx Orders (formerly McD Tech Labs) project, building a voice-based conversational agent for McDonald's drive-thrus in Mountain View, CA. I earned my Masters in Computer Science from Virginia Tech , where I wrote a thesis on Linguistically Differentiating Acts and Recalls of Racial Microaggressions. I was a part of Dr. Eugenia H. Rho's SAIL Research Lab. I was also a member of the Center for Human-Computer Interaction (CHCI) at VT. I received my Bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering in 2021 at Bennett University, India.

During my undergrad, I was a Research Intern at MIDAS Lab at the Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, New Delhi working under the mentorship of Dr. Debanjan Mahata and Dr. Rajiv Ratn Shah. My research focuses on NLP and Computational Social Science, and more recently, Cultural Analytics.
