Ongoing Projects
  • Understanding Acts and Recalls of Racial Microaggressions in Online Social Media Communities
    SAIL Lab, Virgina Tech

    Conducting a study on the phenomenon of Racial Microaggressions in social media platforms such as Reddit, Tumblr, and Twitter through qualitative and quantitative methods. We are creating a seed corpus by collecting accounts of racial MAs as well as recalls on online social media communities. and then qualitatively analyze the corpus using typologies adapted from prior research in social science (Sue et al., 2009) on racial MAs. Finally, we plan to leverage NLP techniques to create a classifier to differentiate acts from recalls.
